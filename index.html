Handmadecorner Seller Automation

Private internal seller tool for my Etsy shop.

This application is used to automate daily shop operations, including order tracking, listing synchronization, personalization workflow, inventory monitoring, and internal production management for custom handmade products.

This app is only used by my shop/team and is not offered to third-party sellers or buyers.
